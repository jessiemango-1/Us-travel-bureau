🌎 US Travel Bureau - History

About the US Travel Bureau

The US Travel Bureau was a government organization during the Great Depression that promoted tourism and domestic travel. Its goal was to boost the economy by encouraging Americans to explore their country.

It provided travel guides, maps, and support for travelers, helping small towns and local businesses.

Fun Facts

  • Travel was a luxury during the Great Depression.
  • The Bureau promoted scenic routes and road trips.
  • Many of its brochures are now historical collectibles.
